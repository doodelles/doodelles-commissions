FAQ's

What types of commissions do you offer?

I offer various types of commissions including sketches, shaded color sketches, and rendered/painted artwork. You can choose from headshots, half-body, and full-body options, all at different price points!

How do I request a commission?

You can request a commission by sending me a DM on Instagram. Please provide me an overview of your request, and we can discuss more in depth when I fully accept the commission.

What is your payment process?

Payment is accepted via CashApp or PayPal. You can choose to pay 100% upfront or 50% as a down payment and the rest after the commission is completed.

Do you offer refunds?

Full refunds are not accepted once I begin your order since by that point I would have already begun the drawing process. If there is any specific reason you need one, please contact me ASAP!

How long will it take to get my commission?

That's dependent on the commission requested, and my own schedule. Commissions on average take anywhere between 1-3 weeks to complete. More detailed and complicated commissions take longer than simple sketch commissions
